FAQ

Do you charge a percentage of salary?

No. Our model is a flat fee per search. The cost is the same whether you hire none or several candidates from the shortlist.

Do you guarantee a hire?

We don’t guarantee a hire. We guarantee a refined shortlist with transparent scoring and notes so you can choose with confidence.

What’s included in the advert “pack”?

Advert creation plus an allocation of ad spend across selected channels. Higher tiers include broader reach and targeted outreach.

Can you handle interviews?

Yes. The Premium plan includes human interviews and detailed assessments. We can also schedule interviews with your team.
