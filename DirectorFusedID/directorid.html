FAQ

Do you replace our team?

No. We extend it. Your people set context and priorities; we bring leadership plus AI capacity to execute the routine with precision.

ISO9001—how quickly can we be audit-ready?

Typical first-time builds take 6–12 weeks, depending on scope and existing artefacts. Maintenance is continuous thereafter.

What does the AI actually do?

Document control, log updates, meeting packs, KPI collection, first-pass drafting—always with human review before anything critical is published.

Where does our data live?

In your systems first. Our AI employees integrate via secure connections and follow your access policies. We can operate entirely inside your tenant if preferred.
