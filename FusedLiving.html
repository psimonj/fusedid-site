Owners and managers need one thing: confidence. That guests can get in, Wi-Fi works, cleaners have access, and the property resets correctly after every stay.

Digital access flows

Separate codes for guests, cleaners and trades with clear start and end times. No key handovers, no lockboxes, no late-night dramas.

Guest Wi-Fi that behaves

A simple guest network kept separate from your own devices, planned for streaming, calls and real-world usage — not lab conditions.

Arrival & departure scenes

Lights, climate and locks set correctly every time. Welcoming on arrival, reset and secure on departure so the place is ready for the next stay.

External cameras, placed right

Coverage focused on entrances and perimeters — privacy-first and practical, with alerts tuned for signal, not noise.

Cleaners & trades, always in

Ongoing codes for regular cleaners and one-off windows for trades, so changeovers run smoothly without you playing gatekeeper.

Remote support when it matters

Help when something goes wrong mid-stay — guidance, resets and remote actions without needing to be on site.

Guest access, handled.

Every stay starts at the front door. FUSED LIVING sets up smart locks and keypads so guests can arrive any time within their booking window — no key handovers, no lockboxes to jam, no late-night “we’re locked out” calls.

Codes are unique per booking, can be time-bound, and can be revoked instantly if plans change. You see who should have access, and when, without needing to be on site.

Cleaners and trades, always in.

Cleaners and maintenance crews need dependable access that doesn’t clash with guest stays. We create separate codes and schedules for non-guest access so everyone can do their job without you playing phone tag.

Regular cleaners can have ongoing weekday codes; ad-hoc trades can have one-off windows. You stay in control of who can enter, and when.

Arrivals that feel welcoming. Departures that reset everything.

We build simple scenes for each changeover. On arrival: pathway lights, key interior lights and climate set to a comfortable baseline. On departure: lights off, certain devices powered down, locks secured.

You decide the rules; we wire the systems behind them. Guests feel looked after, and properties return to a known state ready for the next booking.

Guest Wi-Fi that behaves.

Wi-Fi is the lifeblood of a good review. We design networks with a clear guest Wi-Fi, separated from your owner network, and sized for how the property is actually used.

Guests get simple, printed details; you keep your own devices isolated. Need to restart or check things remotely? We’ll set up sensible tools so you’re not guessing in the dark.

Eyes on the outside, not on your guests.

External cameras and doorbells can provide valuable context after incidents, without turning the property into a surveillance experience. We focus on entrances, driveways and shared approaches — areas where security matters most.

Alerts are tuned for signal, not noise, and recordings support you when there’s a dispute or damage claim. Guests get clear guidance on what’s monitored; you get a calmer, better-protected property.
